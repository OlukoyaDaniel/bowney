What would you do if at age 13 your parents are unable to provide school materials and basic necessities? What would you do if you got accepted into high school but could not go because your parents could not afford?

These are the daunting questions millions of under resourced young girls ask themselves each day in their quest to access good education. Join us in this campaign to achieve our goals in ensuring girls receive good education to create a better world for everyone. Girls own the future of our generation
